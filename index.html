Why TAMARAW

Produce can pass through 5 hands before it reaches you. We cut it down to one.

Every trader, wholesaler, and reseller in between takes a cut and adds a day. By the time it reaches a table, the farmer has earned little and the produce is no longer fresh. TAMARAW connects you straight to the farm, so more of what you pay goes to the person who grew it — and what you get is harvested, not stored.
